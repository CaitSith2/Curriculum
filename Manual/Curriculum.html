On the Subject of Curriculum

I want to major in anything that takes this combination of classes together.

  • You have been lazy through your summer vacation, so taking classes quickly became a matter of life and death- literally.
  • On the bomb there is a grid for your schedule, buttons for the five classes you have to take, and a SUBMIT button.
  • Take the first five characters of the serial number into consideration for the next table. (If the character is a letter, simply convert it to its numeric value.)
Classes you should take
Serial character in the same position is Even PHYSICS PHILOSOPHY PROGRAMMING LINGUISTICS LOGIC
Odd MATHS LITERATURE ECONOMY MANAGEMENT ELECTRONICS
  • These classes are scattered randomly to the 5 buttons presented to you. As a hint, they are marked with their initials as you cycle.
  • Each class has 3 different sections that is available, each of which has 2-3 lectures throughout the week.
  • You should prepare a schedule with the correct classes and with the sections of your choosing. However, by default, you should have zero conflicts.
  • There are also conditions which may or may not affect your schedule:
Name Condition Effect
Band Practice 3 or more batteries present Can't take lectures in the second halves of Monday and Thursday
Sleepy Gary No indicators on the bomb Can't take lectures in the first slot of each day
Bookworm No strikes taken Allowed to have 1 conflict on the schedule
  • There may be more than one correct schedule.
  • When done, press the SUBMIT button.
  • Schedules with too many conflicts will result in a strike.
